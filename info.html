Tournament Information

The registration link for next year's invitational tournament will be posted here on Tuesday September 19, 2017, at 12:30 PM ET. This year, we are happy to announce that the participation fee will be waived for all teams.

Registration Policies

  • Registration cap of 48 teams on a first-register first-serve basis.
  • The first ten schools to successfully sign up requesting two teams can bring both teams. Subsequent schools to register may only bring one team each.
  • Teams will be placed on the waitlist in chronological order after the team quota has been reached
  • All schools must be a registered member of a state Science Olympiad organization

Competition Policies

  • All events will be run by Science Olympiad alumni who have medaled at Nationals or the State level, and all tests will be checked and revised by our executive board for consistency. All tests and keys will be made available to teams after competition.
  • Every team will be assigned a homeroom to stay in during competition day.
  • The tournament schedule will adhere closely to the national tournament schedule.
  • All events will follow the national rules. For events which have different rules or content for different levels of competition, we will adhere to the rules specified for the regional level.
  • Teams may photograph or record videos of their own team's build devices but may not do so for any other team. We will have official photographers take photos that will be released post-tournament.
